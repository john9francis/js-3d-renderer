ThreeJS 3D renderer

By John Francis

Controls

Desktop

Rotate around by clicking and dragging with the mouse. Clicking and dragging while holding shift will move the camera around. Scrolling up or down (or pinching if using a trackpad) will zoom in or out.

Mobile

To rotate, swipe left, right, up, or down. To move, change the "Mode" dropdown to "Move" and then move by swiping left, right, up, or down. To zoom, change the Mode to zoom and then swipe up to zoom in and down to zoom out.
